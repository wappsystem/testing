This survey has been developed to explore the attitudes and experiences of people with chronic low back pain towards pain management, sleep health and readiness to use digital health interventions. Digital health has the potential to make sleep therapies more accessible and affordable and enables possible integration with usual care practices for chronic low back pain. To help us understand how digital health intervention for sleep can best be integrated with physiotherapy practice, we would like to ask you some questions about your lower back pain, sleep, and experiences with digital health.
